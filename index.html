Swami Muktatmananda Saraswati

Pujya Muktatmananda Saraswati with a background of brilliant academic career had studied Vedanta Shashtra from Pujya Swami Dayanandji of Arsha Vidhya Gurukulam Coimbatore and Pujya Swami Viditatmanandji of Adhyatma Vidhya Mandir Thaltej Ahmedabad.

ABOUT

In his Purvashram , Swamiji did his PhD in organic chemistry from Gujarat University. He was having a flourishing business in his professional life, which relinquished some three decades back when he came in contacts with Pujya Swami Viditatmananda Saraswati. He was deeply attracted and moved with the philosophy of ancient Vedanta Shashtra which included Upanishads, Bhagvad Gita many other scriptures. He devoted himself for study of Vedanta under able Guidance of Shree Swami Dayanand Saraswati. He did learn Panini grammar from scholars in Chennai. He learned scriptures in Coimbature with help of commentaries of Shankarcharya. He learned vedic chanting from different pandits in Ahmedabad and Chennai. In order to have futher and deep study swamiji went to Coimbatore in the year 2002 on suggestion and advice from Swami Viditatmananda ji.

SWAMIJI'S EXPERIENCE WITH LIFE

Swami Dayanand Saraswati

In Coimbatore Swamiji continued his passion of studying under Param Pujya Swami Dayanandji maharaj. After Catching his deep desire for relinquishing Samasara for the cause of spreadind Vedanata message to the people at large Pujya Swamiji Dayanandji maharaj bedtowed upon him the “Sanyas Diksha “ and he was given a name of “Swami Muktatmananda Saraswati”

Swami Viditatmananda Saraswati

Under the banner of Adhyatma Vidya Mandir, Swami Viditatmananda conducts various programs to disseminate the knowledge of the scriptures. He gives talks on the Upanishads and the Bhagavad Gita all over Gujarat, India. He also conducts management seminars with a view of showing the relevance of Vedanta in management.

Present

Presently Swami Muktamanandji is busy in studying and Teaching Bhagvad Gita, Upanishads, Brahmasutra, Shrimad Bhagvatam and other Vedic scripture of ancient Saints and Gurus.

Followers

Swamiji has in his followers variety of Sandhaks from different section of society and of all age group including young enthuse, middle aged men and women, house wives, working professionals, senior citizens etc. Swamiji has good control over explaining the apparent senses and the hidden messages of Vedanta by inducide dauguase giving several examples and avecdotes which suits daily life of the peple and threby contributing Transformations in their life.
